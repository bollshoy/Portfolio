About Me

Let's talk a little
bit about me

Hi! My name is Maksym, and I am a Front End developer with a passion
and drive to improve in web technologies. I strive to create web applications
that are not only functional but also visually appealing.

I have more than 10 years of experience in Front End development.
My professional path includes participation in a variety of projects, ranging
from creating dynamic single-page applications to developing interfaces
for large web projects.

I am committed to continuous learning and development, have
creative thinking and attention to detail. I value teamwork, am an effective communicator and problem solver.
